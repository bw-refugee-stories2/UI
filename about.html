About Us

Refugee Stories helps visitors gain a greater understanding of the refugee crisis and what it means to be a refugee. There are more people displaced in the world today than at any time since the end of World War II and it is more important than ever to help people develop empathy for each other. We help people's stories be heard, while providing them with a sense of community and belonging.

Refugee Stories aims to connect volunteers with people in need so we can change the world, one story at a time.
